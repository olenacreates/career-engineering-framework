## Why this product exists

Professionals accumulate years of valuable experience, yet much of it remains fragmented across memories, CVs, project documentation, performance reviews, certifications, presentations, and interview preparation.

As a result, every new job application often starts from scratch instead of building on an evolving body of professional knowledge.

Career Engineering Framework addresses this problem by transforming fragmented career experience into structured, reusable professional knowledge.

## Vision

Career Engineering Framework enables professionals to manage their careers with the same intentionality that engineers apply to building products.

Instead of repeatedly recreating CVs, cover letters, portfolios, and interview stories, professionals maintain a structured knowledge base that evolves throughout their careers.

This knowledge becomes the foundation for career positioning, learning, career decisions, and future opportunities.

## Mission

Helping professionals transform fragmented career experience into structured knowledge, intentional positioning, and informed career decisions.
